About The Program

International Aesthetic & Laser Training Programs

Our training programs are designed for medical professionals who want to expand their expertise in advanced aesthetic medicine. Each course combines rigorous theory with intensive hands-on practice under expert supervision.

  • Comprehensive training for dermatologists and medical doctors seeking to master advanced aesthetic techniques.
  • Hands-on practical sessions with live patients under direct supervision of an international trainer.
  • Evidence-based international protocols aligned with global aesthetic medicine standards.
  • Small batch sizes ensuring personalised attention and maximum skill transfer.
  • International certification provided upon successful course completion.
  • Ongoing post-training mentorship and clinical support.

Why Train With Us

International Trainer

Hands-on Training
