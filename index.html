About the Doctor

Meet Dr. Rahul Pillai

Dr. Rahul Pillai is an internationally acclaimed dermatologist and laser specialist with over 15 years of advanced clinical experience. A Gold Medalist in Dermatology, he pursued specialized training in the USA in cutting-edge laser technologies and aesthetic procedures.

He has delivered lectures and live demonstrations at 30+ international dermatology conferences across Asia, Europe, and the Americas, and is a recognized trainer for aesthetic physicians and dermatologists worldwide. His patient base spans celebrities, healthcare professionals, and patients from over 20 countries.
