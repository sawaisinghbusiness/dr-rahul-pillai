About The Doctor

Meet Dr Rahul Pillai

Dr Rahul Pillai is an internationally recognized dermatologist and aesthetic surgeon with over 15 years of clinical excellence. A proud Gold Medalist in MD Dermatology, he received advanced training in laser and cosmetic dermatology in the USA, bringing world-class techniques to his patients.

As a sought-after international speaker, Dr Pillai has addressed over 50 conferences globally and is an active member of prestigious dermatology associations. His clinic is equipped with the latest FDA-approved laser technologies, ensuring the safest and most effective outcomes.
