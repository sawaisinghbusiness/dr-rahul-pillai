Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is laser treatment safe for all skin types?
Yes. Our clinic uses advanced multi-wavelength lasers that are calibrated for all Fitzpatrick skin types, including darker Indian skin tones. Every treatment begins with a thorough consultation and test patch to ensure complete safety and suitability.
How many sessions will I need?
The number of sessions varies depending on the treatment and your individual skin concern. Laser hair removal typically requires 6–8 sessions, while pigmentation or scar treatments may need 4–6 sessions. Dr. Pillai will provide a personalised treatment plan during your consultation.
Is there any downtime after treatment?
Most treatments such as laser hair removal, HydraFacial and PRP have no downtime at all. Deeper treatments like fractional CO2 laser or chemical peels may have 3–7 days of mild redness or peeling. Dr. Pillai will advise you on what to expect before beginning your treatment.
Are the results permanent?
Results vary by treatment. Laser hair removal offers permanent reduction in hair growth. Scar and pigmentation treatments offer long-lasting improvement. Botox and fillers are temporary (6–18 months). Hair transplants are permanent. Maintenance sessions may be recommended for optimal longevity.
Are the treatments painful?
Patient comfort is our priority. Most laser treatments cause only mild warmth or tingling. We apply topical anaesthetic cream before procedures where needed. Injections like Botox and fillers are performed with the finest needles to minimise discomfort. The majority of patients find the procedures very well tolerated.
How do I prepare for my first treatment?
For most laser treatments, avoid sun exposure and self-tanning products for 2–4 weeks prior. Do not wax or thread the area. Come to your appointment with clean, product-free skin. Specific preparation instructions will be given after your consultation based on your chosen treatment.
